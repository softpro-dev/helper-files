Price Logic

 
1. When submitting a vehicle rental request to the fleet owner, send an SMS to the fleet owner’s phone and initiate a notification. If the driver and the fleet owner are the same person (determined by phone number), send a different type of message and notification to approve their own car for themselves.

2. Add a rejectable feature to the app:fleeter -> Rental -> Requests Section (from the details view). Display the driver’s information and add a “Call to Driver” button. After confirming or rejecting the car, send an SMS and initiate a notification to the driver. However, if the phone number is the same, meaning the driver and the fleet owner are the same person, handle the message and notification text accordingly.


Pricing Logic

3. After confirming a vehicle rental, if the driver wants to cancel, a minimum charge will be added based on the following criteria:
- If the rental period has not yet started, a minimum charge of 1 day will be added.
- If the rental period has started but is not yet over 1 week, a minimum charge of 1 week will be added.
- If the rental period has started and is not yet over 2 weeks, a minimum charge of 2 weeks will be added.
- If the rental period has started and is not yet over 3 weeks, a minimum charge of 3 weeks will be added.
- If the rental period has started and is not yet over 4 weeks, a minimum charge of 1 month will be added.
